About Aquagrow

A digital home for your feed formulation work

AQUAGROW is a digital platform developed to support fish feed production, ingredient management, and feed formulation. It is used internally for feed production while also providing tools for farmers to explore and formulate nutritionally balanced fish feeds.

Designed around the real growth cycle of Nile Tilapia, Aquagrow keeps every formulation aligned to FAO nutritional guidance across the Fry, Juvenile, and Grower stages — so farm teams can trust the numbers before feed ever reaches the tank.

Key Features

Everything a feed formulation team needs

A focused toolset built around how farms actually plan and produce feed.

Ingredient Management

Keep a single source of truth for every ingredient — nutrition profile, cost, and stock level, always current.

Automated Nutrient Calculations

Protein, lipid, ash, and fibre totals are calculated the moment ingredients and quantities are set.

Feed Stage Validation

Every formulation is checked against FAO reference ranges for Fry, Juvenile, and Grower stages.

Save, Edit & Delete

Full control over every formulation and ingredient record, with confirmation before anything is removed.

Real-Time Updates

Change a quantity and watch nutrient totals and compliance status update instantly, no recalculation needed.
